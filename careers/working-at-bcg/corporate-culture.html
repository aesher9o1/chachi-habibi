    Predictability, Teaming, and Open Communication

    Working at BCG means working differently. We seek a better way to manage case work, and helping our consultants sustain longer, more satisfying careers is a constant objective.

    To reach this goal, we created a global program we call PTO (Predictability, Teaming, and Open Communication).

    What is PTO?

    Led by an experienced BCG facilitator, PTO provides a forum for sharing each team member’s progress and needs and establishes a detailed roadmap—from launch to completion—for every project. The roadmap includes transparent working norms, priorities, and a collectively agreed-on time-off goal for each team member, so that low-value work doesn’t consume your high-value personal and professional time.

    PTO helps you:

    • BUILD an understanding of how to manage complex projects
    • CONNECT deeply with colleagues and clients
    • GROW skills needed to advance, such as teamwork and leadership

    The Impact of PTO

    BCGers report exceptional improvements in both personal satisfaction and project performance after adopting PTO:

    • An average 35% increase in teamwork and collaboration
    • A 35% increase in value delivered to clients
    • A 100% increase in team effectiveness

    Over the past decade, we’ve continued to evolve the program. Hear from PTO’s founder, Leslie Perlow, on how PTO has improved the way BCG’s teams work, learn, and drive client value—and learn from BCGers about strategies toward career sustainability
