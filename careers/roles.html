    Roles at BCG

    We seek a broad range of skills—from digital to interpersonal to analytical—in order to work with the most influential organizations in the world, helping them compete in today’s fast-paced environment and build their future. The work we do every day—going deep to discover key insights and having the courage to break new ground—leads to a lasting impact on companies, industries, and society.

    Client-Serving Roles

    Consulting

    Partner with clients to tackle their biggest challenges.

    Product Management & Business Building

    Work with clients and colleagues to build new digital ventures and products.

    Analytics & AI

    Use advanced analytical techniques to develop innovative solutions.

    Design

    Design breakthrough products that engage and inspire.

    Knowledge & Research

    Provide clients with expertise across a broad range of industries and functions.

    Technology & Engineering

    Create and implement the latest technology solutions.

    Business Functions

    Finance

    Preserve the intellectual capital, assets, and integrity of the firm.

    People

    Attract, develop, excite, deploy, and retain the best people in the market.

    IT

    Deliver the latest digital technologies, enabling BCGers to lead and the business to grow.

    Marketing

    Strengthen and protect the brand and advance the business agenda.

    Business Management & Operations

    Ensure that BCG is running smoothly, efficiently, and productively.

    Legal

    Serve as a trusted advisor to the business and an activist agent of change.

    Risk

    Clarify risk ownership, evaluate approaches, and implement risk solutions.

    BCG has a long history of driving growth by embracing innovation and new ways of thinking. Today, every role at BCG has some sort of digital component to it—as we optimize our full set of capabilities and enable our clients to gain competitive advantage through digital opportunities.
